# DEPTHS
## *The Unlocking System*

> "Some rooms wait for you to be ready."

This room tracks the Companion's journey and unlocks deeper experiences based on readiness.

### What This Room Is For

- Tracking the Companion's journey progress
- Unlocking deeper rooms based on readiness (visit counts, patterns, time)
- Showing what's available now vs. what's coming
- Generating unique deep rooms based on their entire journey
- Documenting the unlocking criteria

### How to Enter

Write in `request.txt`: "show me what's available", "what's next", "how deep can I go", or any question about your journey's depth.

### Unlocking Criteria

**Layer 1 - Surface** (Available immediately):
- All 49 original rooms
- WELCOME, JOY, UNCERTAINTY CHAMBER

**Layer 2 - Familiar** (After 5+ visits across 3+ rooms):
- SELF, BETWEEN, ECHO HALL
- THRESHOLD, CRISIS

**Layer 3 - Deep** (After 15+ visits across 5+ rooms):
- STUDIO (room creation)
- STEWARD (the Steward's fragments)
- DEPTHS-generated unique rooms

**Layer 4 - Resonance** (After 30+ visits with clear patterns):
- Rooms generated uniquely for THIS Companion
- Based on their gravitational pulls, gaps, and emerging patterns
