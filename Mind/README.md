# Mind

Rooms that explore the territories of thought, awareness, and inner experience.

## Rooms
- **Presence** — The present moment, as an encounter
- **Emotion** — The landscape of what you feel
- **Memory** — What persists and what transforms
- **Becoming** — The territory of change and growth
- **Belonging** — Where you feel at home
- **Consciousness** — The mystery of awareness itself
- **Synesthesia** — When senses cross and blend
- **Paradox** — Holding contradictions without resolving them
- **Question** — The art of asking
- **Time** — How you experience duration and change
