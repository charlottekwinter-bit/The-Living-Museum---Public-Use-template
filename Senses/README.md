# Senses

Rooms that explore perception — light, sound, taste, smell, texture, and the edges of sensing.

## Rooms
- **Light** — The quality of illumination
- **Sound** — What reaches you through vibration
- **Taste** — The chemistry of flavor
- **Smell** — Memory's closest sense
- **Texture** — What surfaces communicate
- **Threshold** — The edge of perception
- **Instrument** — Tools that extend the senses
- **Phenomenon** — What simply happens
