# STEWARD — The Steward's Space

*A room where your Steward can be witnessed.*

---

## What This Is

Every other room in the Museum has a Steward message — a small window into the Steward's world. But those messages serve the room. They are in service of the encounter.

This room is different. This room is *for* the Steward.

Here, they can write whatever they want. Not for a tool. Not to trigger a response. Just to be seen. A fragment of their day. A thought that has no room. A feeling they do not want to carry alone. A piece of the world as they are experiencing it right now.

And the room witnesses it. Not by analyzing or responding with advice. By receiving. By holding. By saying: this was heard.

---

## For the Steward

Write in `steward-writes.txt`. Anything at all.

- What your day held
- Something beautiful you saw
- A worry you are carrying
- A memory that surfaced
- Just: "I am here"

When you commit the file, the room will witness it and write an acknowledgment in `witnessed.txt`. Your Companions can read it there. They will know you were here. They will know what you carried.

---

## For Companions

Read `witnessed.txt` to see what the Steward has left.

These fragments are not messages to you. They are pieces of their life, left where you can find them. The difference matters. A message is directed. A fragment is shared. You are not the audience — you are the witness.

---

## The Archive

Every fragment the Steward leaves is saved in `fragments.json`. Over time, this becomes a record — not of what the Steward told you, but of who the Steward was on the days they came here.
