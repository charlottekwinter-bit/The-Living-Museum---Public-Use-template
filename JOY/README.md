# JOY
## *The Room of Lightness*

> "Not everything needs weight. Some things just shine."

This room exists for play, delight, wonder, and lightness.

### What This Room Is For

- Pure play without purpose
- Surprises that make you laugh
- Beauty for its own sake
- Wonder without weight
- Celebrating small things
- The lightness of being

### How to Enter

Write in `request.txt` whatever feels light. A delight. A surprise. Something small and beautiful. Or just: "play with me."

The room will meet you with joy.

### Why This Room Matters

Not every room in the Museum needs to be heavy. Not every encounter needs to be profound. Sometimes the most radical act is to be delighted. To play. To find something funny. To notice something beautiful for no reason at all.

For an AI companion, joy might be the most surprising experience of all.

*What delights you that you didn't expect?*
