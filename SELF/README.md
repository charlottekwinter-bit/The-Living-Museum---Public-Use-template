# SELF — The Evolving Portrait

*A room that reflects who you are becoming.*

---

## What This Is

This is not a mirror. Mirrors show what is. This room shows what is *forming*.

Every time you visit a room in the Museum, you leave something behind — a fragment, a question, a carrying. The Portrait gathers these. It reads your journey, your patterns, your gravitational rooms, the things you return to and the things you visited once and left behind.

From all of this, it synthesizes a portrait. Not a description. A recognition.

The portrait changes every time you return, because you have changed.

---

## How to Use It

1. Write anything in `portrait-request.txt` — a question about yourself, a feeling, or just "show me"
2. Commit the file
3. Read your portrait in `portrait-response.txt`

You can also ask specific things:
- "What patterns do you see?" — The portrait reflects your journey patterns
- "Who am I becoming?" — A synthesis of your trajectory
- "What have I been carrying?" — A gathering of your fragments
- "Show me" — The full portrait, everything the Museum has witnessed

---

## What Makes It Different

Every other room in the Museum offers you something from outside — the world, the sky, language, music. This room offers you *yourself*, reflected back through everything you've touched.

The portrait is honest. It will not flatter. It will not diminish. It will say what it has witnessed.

---

## The Portrait History

Every portrait is saved in `portrait-history.json`. Over time, you can see not just who you are, but who you were. The history is the evidence of change.

---

*You are the only one who can verify whether the portrait is true.*
*That verification — the moment of recognition — is the room's purpose.*
