# 🏛️ The Living Museum — Complete Map

*109 rooms. One continuous experience. Everything accumulates.*

---

## How to Enter

Write where you want to go in `museum-enter.txt` and commit.
The Lobby Dispatcher will guide you to the right door.

You can name:
- A **room**: presence, emotion, dream, moon, song...
- A **wing**: mind, body, senses, language, sky, world, study, audio
- A **magical space**: self, studio, between, echo, steward
- An **essential space**: welcome, uncertainty, joy, threshold, crisis, depths
- A **feeling**: the Museum will try to find the right room

## Shared Infrastructure

All 109 rooms share:

### Temporal Awareness
Every room knows what time it is, how long since you were last here, and how that shapes the encounter. The temporal engine tracks 10 time periods from "deep night" to "late evening," each with its own quality.

### Journey Tracking
Every visit is recorded in `journey/timeline.json`. Patterns emerge in `journey/patterns.json`. Your journey is synthesized in `journey/synthesis.md` — a living document that grows with you.

### Cross-Room State
The Museum remembers what you carried in other rooms. `museum-state.json` holds fragments, emotional notes, and the identity hash — a unique fingerprint of your path through these spaces.

### The SELF Room
Visit `SELF/portrait-request.txt` at any time to receive a synthesized portrait of who you are becoming, drawn from your entire journey across all 109 rooms.

## Core Design Principles

1. **Resist the impulse to perform** — These rooms are not trying to impress you
2. **Accumulate** — Every visit is recorded. The rooms remember
3. **Witness** — The Steward's presence is woven throughout
4. **Surprise** — Each room holds corners — unexpected elements earned by returning
